About Us

Any human that frequents the internet has encountered digital “cookies.” Very few of us read the privacy policies or fully understand what the cookies are doing. Yet, some of these policies are not a friend. Within the fine print, there can be gross violations of personal privacy and security from selling one’s data to forever haunting their internet existence.

Thus, we create B4UAgree: a browser extension plug-in that reads, analyzes, and summarizes any privacy policy one encounters on the web. We cannot change how companies operate with their cookies, but we can change how one perceives these cookies. We hope to create users who are fully aware of a company’s intentions and usage of their data. Therefore, they can be empowered to make the decisions that are best for them.

Our Process

At B4UAgree, we employ a meticulous process to provide users with comprehensive yet concise summaries and accurate scores of privacy policies. Our summarization technique harnesses the power of SBert, a state-of-the-art language processing model, renowned for its ability to distill complex textual information into succinct summaries. Leveraging SBert's advanced capabilities, we meticulously analyze and extract the essence of each privacy policy, ensuring that users receive clear and digestible insights.

The scoring process involves parsing privacy policy text into statements, identifying privacy-related terms, and calculating scores based on their occurrence and significance within each statement. These scores are then aggregated and normalized to assess the overall quality and transparency of the policy. Continuous refinement ensures the scoring methodology remains accurate and relevant over time.
